Changed navigation bar on mobile

September 18, 2018

I have redesigned the collapsible navigation bar on mobile, with some minor changes to the desktop navigation.

On mobile browsers, the collapsible navigation bar is much more rounded & more apparent to the user. The navigation bar is also no longer always on top of the page, and will scroll with the user as well.

Meanwhile, the navigation bar for desktop is moved from overhead to a sidebar, and is accessible at any moment for desktop viewers!

EDIT 1: I have also changed up the navigation bar's, header's, and headings' fontstyles from Noto Serif to Open Sans - this allows for a cleaner, more modernized look while not being distracting from the main text's Noto Serif!

Working on Weight-Tracking PWA

September 14, 2018

I am currently designing a progressive webapp (PWA) to input, track, and predict weight logs of a person.

In the draft, I have included a navbar (for navigating to other pages in the PWA), a graph of date/time against current weight (with max boundary being the largest weight rounded up to nearest integer, and the min boundary being the smallest weight rounded down to nearest integer), a section for scrollable table of date, time, & weight, and a floating action button (also known as FAB in Android applications) which opens a page for user to input weight.

It is currently in the drafting stage, but it should come to fruition soon.
